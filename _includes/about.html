About Me

Hello everyone! I’m Iacopo, a young Italian born in 1990.
I created this website to gather the things I do and read (hoping my laziness won’t stop me from keeping it updated).
I was born and raised in Italy, so I apologize in advance for my English.
As a hip-hop fanatic, I’d say my childhood was tough, but it wasn’t:D. I used to spend my afternoons with my friends until middle school.
In high school, I started studying computer science, which is now my main passion. Until then, I was a lazy student who always avoided reading, but thanks to computer science, I started reading manuals, and gradually, I began to appreciate less technical books as well.
Currently, I work as a developer and teach computer science to high school students. I enjoy practicing almost all sports, watching movies, reading, listening to podcasts, taking walks, and listening to music!
