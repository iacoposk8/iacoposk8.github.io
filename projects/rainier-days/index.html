The Rainiest Days (When Not to Wash the Car)



Place the mouse over a line to find out the day; the darker it is, the more historically likely it is to rain.
The data was collected for Varese (the province where I live) over 10 years, from 2007 to 2017.
If you also want to create your own version, you can download this code that uses PHP and SQLite
